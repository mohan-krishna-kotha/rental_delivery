📦 RentEx Delivery Partner

Quick Start Guide

🚀 Getting Started

Welcome to the RentEx Delivery Partner app! This guide will help you understand how to use all the features of the app.

🔐 Login

Step 1: Access the Login Page

When you first open the app, you'll see the login page with:

  • Email or Phone Number field
  • Password field
  • Forgot Password link
  • Sign In button

Demo Mode

For testing: Enter any email/phone and password, then click "Sign In" to access the dashboard.

🏠 Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you'll land on the main dashboard with:

Statistics Cards

  • Today's Earnings: Money earned today (₹1,250)
  • Completed: Number of deliveries completed (8)
  • Active: Ongoing deliveries (2)
  • Rewards: Points earned (850 pts)

Order List

View all your upcoming delivery requests. Each order shows:

  • Order ID and status badge
  • Item name and description
  • Pickup and drop-off locations
  • Package size and weight
  • Earnings for this delivery
  • Accept/Reject buttons

📦 Managing Orders

Accepting an Order

  1. Review the order details on the dashboard
  2. Click the green "✓ Accept" button
  3. You'll be taken to the order details page
  4. A success notification will appear

Rejecting an Order

  1. Click the "✕ Reject" button on any order
  2. Confirm your decision
  3. The order will be removed from your list

📋 Order Details Page

This is the most important page for active deliveries. It contains:

1. Order Information

  • Item name and description
  • Package size and weight
  • Your earnings
  • Rental period (for rental items)

2. Seller Information

  • Name and avatar
  • Rating and rental history
  • Pickup address
  • Contact phone number

3. Customer Information

  • Name and avatar
  • Rating and member status
  • Delivery address
  • Contact phone number

📸 Photo Verification

Important: You must capture a photo of the item before completing delivery!

How to Capture Photos

  1. Scroll to the "Item Verification Photo" section
  2. Click the "📷 Capture Photo" button
  3. Your device camera will open
  4. Take a clear photo of the item
  5. The photo will appear in the preview area
  6. A success notification will confirm the capture

🗺️ Navigation & Maps

Route Preview

The app shows a route preview with:

  • Distance (e.g., 8.5 km)
  • Estimated Time of Arrival (ETA: 25 mins)
  • Traffic conditions

Google Maps Integration

  1. Click "🧭 Open in Google Maps" button
  2. Google Maps will open in a new tab
  3. Turn-by-turn directions will be displayed
  4. Follow the route to complete your delivery

🔍 Rental Item Comparison

For rental returns: Compare the item's condition before and after the rental period.

How to Use

  1. Click "🔍 Show Condition Comparison" button
  2. View the "Before Rental" photo (taken at initial delivery)
  3. Capture an "After Rental" photo
  4. Compare both images for any damage
  5. If damage is found, report it before completing delivery

✅ Completing a Delivery

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Verify: Ensure you've captured the required photos
  2. Confirm: Click the green "✓ Complete Delivery" button
  3. Approve: Confirm the completion in the popup
  4. Celebrate: Watch the confetti animation! 🎉
  5. Reward: See your earnings and bonus points

What Happens After Completion

  • Your earnings are updated (+₹180)
  • Completed deliveries count increases
  • Reward points are added (+50 pts)
  • Active deliveries count decreases
  • The order moves to your history

⚠️ Reporting Issues

If you encounter any problems during delivery:

  1. Click the "⚠️ Report Issue" button
  2. Select the type of issue:
    • Item is damaged
    • Item is different from description
    • Customer is unavailable
    • Address is incorrect
    • Other issue
  3. Support team will be notified immediately
  4. Wait for assistance before proceeding

🧭 Bottom Navigation

Quickly access different sections of the app:

  • 🏠 Home: Dashboard with statistics and orders
  • 📦 Orders: View all your deliveries
  • 💰 Earnings: Track your income and rewards
  • 👤 Profile: Manage your account settings

💡 Pro Tips

Maximize Your Earnings

  • Accept orders quickly to get priority
  • Complete deliveries on time for bonus points
  • Maintain high ratings for better opportunities
  • Use Google Maps for fastest routes

Photo Best Practices

  • Take photos in good lighting
  • Capture the entire item clearly
  • Show any existing damage for protection
  • Store photos are kept for 30 days

Safety First

  • Always verify the customer's identity
  • Check package contents before pickup
  • Report suspicious items immediately
  • Use the in-app phone feature for privacy

🎯 Quick Actions Checklist

For Every Delivery:

  1. ☑️ Accept the order from dashboard
  2. ☑️ Review seller and customer details
  3. ☑️ Capture pickup verification photo
  4. ☑️ Navigate using Google Maps
  5. ☑️ (For rentals) Compare before/after photos
  6. ☑️ Complete delivery and collect rewards

❓ FAQs

Q: What if I can't capture a photo?

The app requires a photo for verification. If you're having camera issues, contact support immediately.

Q: Can I cancel an accepted order?

Yes, but excessive cancellations may affect your rating. Use the "Report Issue" feature to explain.

Q: How long are photos stored?

Photos are stored for 30 days to help resolve any disputes.

Q: When do I get paid?

Earnings are updated in real-time. Actual payments are processed weekly to your bank account.

Q: What are reward points for?

Reward points can be redeemed for bonuses, fuel discounts, and exclusive perks!
